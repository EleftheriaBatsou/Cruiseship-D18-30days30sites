DISCOVERY AT SEA

Hit DiscoveryTM & Animal PlanetTM shows come to life on board Princess Cruises ships.
Princess Cruises and Discovery Communications are teaming up to create new memorable vacation experiences for the whole family to enjoy. Come back captivated with the new Stargazing experience; activities featuring Discovery hit shows such as MythBusters, Deadliest Catch, and Shark Week; plus Discovery and Animal Planet™ shore excursions. And, our all new Style at Sea℠ featuring fashion expert tips and tricks.
STARGAZING WITH DISCOVERY AT SEA

Science Channel and Princess Cruises will take you on a cosmic voyage as you look to the skies from our top deck. On this interactive guided tour, a Princess Stargazing Specialist will teach you how to find your bearings and identify the major constellations, hear the secrets of the stars and the folklore around them. In addition, our onboard libraries will be stocked with supplementary astronomy resources for guests to enjoy and continue their exploration of the night's sky when returning home. Great for all ages.
Learn more about Discovery at Sea’s Science Channel Ambassador astrophysicist Dr. Hakeem Oluseyi.
Contingent upon weather and light conditions. Excludes Alaska itineraries due to the midnight sun.
ENCOUNTERS WITH DISCOVERY AT SEA

Learn the stories behind the destinations you’ll visit from regional residents who know the areas personally. Find out about native plants and animals from naturalists who have studied them for years. And gain insights into events from years past that shape the world we see today. Every Encounters onboard speaker offers enriching discoveries that add to the immersive experience through which Princess Cruises inspires you to come back new.
Learn more about Encounters with Discovery at Sea.
DISCOVERY & ANIMAL PLANET SHORE EXCURSIONS

We are proud to offer shore excursions that are not only fascinating experiences for the whole family, but recommended by two of the most trusted names in exploration – Discovery and Animal Planet.
Discovery and Animal Planet Exclusive Tours are unique tours that cannot be booked independently and are available only through Princess Cruises. Discovery Recommended Tours provide enriching and immersive once-in-a-lifetime experiences, while Animal Planet Recommended Tours show you the coolest animals around the globe.
